Copycat Killbot

July 13, 2014 in category Jams and tags unity3d

By Robert Utter

You control an unarmed ship, facing off against the eponymous Copycat Killbot and its deadly lasers. You cannot hope to damage the Killbot directly, but because it mirrors your moves exactly you may be able to trick it into ramming mines that are in the area.

Copycat Killbot is a @3hrgamejam submission I made with Megan Ting for the theme "OPPOSITES".

Try the game for yourself!
